Welcome to the Mud

700 Acres of Pure Adventure

Wheelin' in the Country is a premier off-road park located in beautiful Summertown, Tennessee. Spanning approximately 700 acres, our park offers diverse terrain guaranteed to thrill riders of all skill levels—from novice beginners to expert off-roaders.

Tackle our famous deep mud holes, navigate scenic ponds, conquer steep climbs, and explore winding valleys. We host epic weekend riding events designed for maximum fun and community spirit.

Allowed Vehicles

  • ATVs & UTVs
  • Jeeps
  • Dirt Bikes
  • Dune Buggies
  • * Note: No street-legal only vehicles allowed.

Camping

Free primitive camping available for weekend riders.

RV Sites

RV hookup sites available for $25/night.

Lodging

Cabins and bunkhouses available to rent.

Amenities

Hot showers, restrooms, concession stand & repair shop.

Admission & Pricing

  • Weekend Pass (Ages 16+) $30
  • Daily Pass (Ages 16+) $20
  • Kids (15 & under) Free
  • RV Site $25/night
  • Primitive Camping Free

Park Rules

For the safety and enjoyment of all guests, the following rules are strictly enforced:

  • DOT Helmets Required Mandatory for all riders on ATVs, dirt bikes, and open-cab vehicles.
  • No Alcohol Zero tolerance policy for alcohol consumption while riding or on the trails.
  • Sound Limit: 96db Vehicles must adhere to the 96 decibel sound restriction.
  • Armbands Mandatory Armbands must be worn and visible at all times while in the park.
